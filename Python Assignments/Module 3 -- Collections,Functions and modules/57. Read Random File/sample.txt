India is a diverse and culturally rich country, known for its vast heritage and traditions. It is the seventh-largest country globally, both in terms of area and population. India shares its borders with several countries and is a secular and democratic nation that respects all religions. Mahatma Gandhi, known for his non-violent approach, is revered as the father of the nation. The national flag, known as Tiranga, symbolizes the country's unity. India is a land of festivals, varied dressing styles, and diverse cuisines, where people of different backgrounds coexist harmoniously, showcasing the essence of 'Unity in Diversity.'
